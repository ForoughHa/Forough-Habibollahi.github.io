I am a data scientist and computational neuroscience postdoctoral research fellow at Cortical Labs. Our work focuses on creating biological intelligence by fusing living brain cells with computing devices.

Currently, I am a full-time data scientist and computational neuroscience postdoctoral research fellow at Cortical Labs. We are a start-up company that has created DishBrain, which fuses living brain cells onto computing devices to create machines with biological intelligence.
